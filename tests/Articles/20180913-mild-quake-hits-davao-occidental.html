MILD QUAKE HITS DAVAO OCCIDENTAL A mild quake hit Sarangani, Davao Occidental early Friday morning, the Philippine Institute of Volcanology and Seismology (PHIVOLCS) reported. The epicenter is 406 kilometers southeast of Sarangani with a depth of 162 kilometers and tectonic in origin. There are no expected damage after the quake and no aftershocks. - BAP, GMA NEWS
